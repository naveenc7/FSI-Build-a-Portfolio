About Me

Hello prospective reader, my name is Md Naveen Chowdhury and I am an aspiring software developer. I am currently 22 years old and am super excited to learn more about coding and software development. I went to Vassar College for my under-grad pursuing a Political Science degree. Whilst that learning environment was fulfilling and enriching in many regards, I found out that it truly was not for me. Learning to work with computers and designing my own work is very interesting, and I want to find different avenues where I can express myself through my work. I am currently enrolled in the NYU SPS Software Development Intro Bootcamp and this initial portfolio is an assignment for that class. I guess this first portfolio will provide myself with a template of what is to come forward -- hopefully some progress and better skills to decorate my portfolio. My end-goal for this bootcamp is to learn all the necessary skills required in order to create and develop an app idea of my own; maybe that project in itself makes myself visible to employers at the end of the Extended Course bootcamp cycle. Fingers crossed. Logging out now. Peace.
